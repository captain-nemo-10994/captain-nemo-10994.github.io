Do not spare yourself from heartfelt prayer even when you
have spent the whole day in hard work. Do not indulge in
laziness when you pray; tell God everything that is in 
your heart. If you allow yourself time to pray with 
diligence, you will not fall asleep before you have wept 
over your sins. Believe that, if for the sake of bodily 
rest you pray hurriedly, you will lose the tranquility of
both body and soul. By what labor, sweat and tears is 
our closeness to God achieved! 
                   ~ St. John of Kronstadt
